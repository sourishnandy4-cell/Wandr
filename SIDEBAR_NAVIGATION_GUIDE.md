## 📄 How to Add Documents

1. Click **"Docs"** in sidebar
2. Click **"Add Document"** button
3. Fill in:
   - Document name (e.g., "Flight Ticket")
   - Link/URL (where the document is stored)
   - Category (Flight, Hotel, Insurance, Visa, Other)
4. Click **"Add Document"**
5. Document appears as a card
6. Click **"Open"** to view the document
7. Click trash icon to delete
